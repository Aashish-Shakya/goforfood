About Us

Launched in 2021, GO 4 FOOD offers mobile and online meal ordering capabilities to restaurants in thousands of cities in the India. GO 4 FOOD provides digital menus on websites and in its app, from which customers can securely pay with the most options available out of all restaurant e-commerce systems.
